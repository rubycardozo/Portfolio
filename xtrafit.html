Xtrafit

XtraFit is a fitness company based in NYC. For this project, we designed a fitness app that allows users to track their fitness and stay motivated. Users can log their data, create exercise, sleep and nutrition goals, as well as see a clear overview of their progress.

Our Goals

To allow users to get an overview of all their fitness progress To allow users to set goals, track progress, and share the progress with others inside and outside of the platform To motivate our users to create and maintain healthy fitness habits

Research

We surveyed 15 participants in order to get a better understanding of what users needed assistance with when it came to their fitness. We asked them question such as:

Quantitative

  • How often do you workout?
  • Do you track your steps?
  • Do you maintain a food log?

Qualitative

  • Do you use a fitness app and why?
  • How valuable is fitness tracking to you?
  • How do you hold yourself accountable?

Reseach Results

67% participants had a fitness related goal to improve their health
53% Participants worked out on a weekly basis
47% Participants actively tracked their fitness progress and felt that tracking milestones was crucial to their health.
87% Participants have used a fitness app in the past
60% Participants actively use a fitness app for tracking
40% Participants preferred sharing their workout progress with their community

Analysis

We analyzed our survey results in order to find out our users top challenges. We identified the following key categories that impacted our target audience:

  • 1. Motivation: A large majority of the participants were looking for a solution that made them excited to partake in fitness.
  • 2. Nutrition: The participants felt that food tracking was a key part of their success in the fitness journey.
  • 3. Community & Sharing: The participants were lacking accountability which hindered their continuous fitness progress.
